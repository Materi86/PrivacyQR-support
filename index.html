Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. PrivacyQR works 100% offline. All your QR codes and settings are stored locally on your device. We never upload any data to external servers.

What does "Burn After Read" mean?

When enabled, a QR code will automatically be deleted from your library after it's been viewed once. Perfect for sharing temporary information securely.

What is the Kill Switch?

The Kill Switch allows you to instantly deactivate a saved QR code, making it appear invalid if someone tries to use it.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchases. Your Pro features will be restored using your Apple ID.

Is Face ID/Touch ID data stored by the app?

No. Biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS. PrivacyQR never accesses or stores any biometric data.
